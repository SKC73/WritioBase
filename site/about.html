About our team

Sean Koji Callaghan, B.Ed., M.A., Ph.D.

Sean received his B.Ed. and M.A. from the U of A, and his doctoral degree in Japanese literature from the U of T. He spent his early years writing and directing plays, one of which he got the opportunity to workshop with the Queensland Theatre Company. Another of his plays was published with NeWest Press. He has published short fiction with Existere, the Journal of Arts and Literature and Microfiction Monday Magazine. He has a short story forthcoming from Grain Magazine. He has taught students of all ages from primary students to 3rd-year undergrads to adult learners.

What we are about

There are classes about writing by writers for writers. There are classes by teachers for students who are writers. This is a website for writers by teachers who are writers and writers who are teachers. Which means, it's a website for anyone who wants to learn to write without feeling intimidated by the teacher.
